Nearby verified pros bid in real time — you choose on price, rating and schedule.

We can hail rides, stream movies and order groceries with absolute control over price at the tap of a button.

Yet the moment a pipe bursts, or we need a simple haircut at home — we're thrown back into the dark ages.

Problem

The home-services market runs on three broken defaults.

1

The pricing monopoly

Existing apps dictate arbitrary rates, stripping control from both customers and service providers.

2

The hourly billing trap

No upfront flat rates mean simple repairs turn into open-ended, unpredictable financial liabilities.

3

The "blind doorstep" risk

Customers pay premium surge fees for urgent help, yet have zero say in the quality or identity of the professional who shows up.

Solution

One app. Two engines. Total control.

ZappServ splits the market by how people actually decide: negotiate complex jobs on price, or book simple ones instantly.

Engine 1

Real-Time Bid Trades

How it works

Post a complex job — plumbing, electrical — and watch nearby pros bid in real time.

The payoff

Absolute price transparency, instant dispatch, and complete cost control.

Engine 2

On-Demand Lifestyle Booking

How it works

Browse, compare and see who's closest on the live map. Instantly book wellness, pet care and barber services.

The payoff

Choose the perfect provider by rating, price and schedule — on your own terms.

Business Model

Simple, transparent monetization on both engines.

Take rate

Fixed commission

A fixed cut on every completed bid-trade and lifestyle booking transacted through the platform.

Per booking

Service fee

A flat service fee charged to the customer at checkout, on top of the winning bid or listed price.

Exact commission and fee structure to be finalized during pilot — priced to stay below traditional callout markups while sustaining unit economics.
