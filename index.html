The Gap

We can hail a ride with absolute price control. So why does a burst pipe still send us into the dark ages?

Everywhere else

Rides, movies, groceries — booked and priced at the tap of a button, with total transparency before we commit.

Home & lifestyle services

The moment a pipe bursts or we need a haircut at home, we're back to phone calls, guesswork, and no price control at all.

Problem Statement

Four things are broken about how home & lifestyle services get booked.

01

The Pricing Monopoly

Existing apps dictate arbitrary rates, stripping control from both customers and service providers.

02

The Hourly Billing Trap

No upfront flat rates mean simple repairs turn into open-ended, unpredictable financial liabilities.

03

The "Blind Doorstep" Risk

No trust signal — customers can't verify a pro's identity, skill, or past work before a stranger enters their home or takes their money.

04

Fragmented Supply

Electricians, salons, pet sitters — every category lives on a different app, so there's no single place to find and book trusted help.

Solution

One app. Two engines. Total control.

Engine 1

Real-Time Bid Trades

How it works: Post a complex job — plumbing, electrical — and watch nearby pros bid in real time.
The payoff: Absolute price transparency, instant dispatch, and complete cost control.
Engine 2

On-Demand Lifestyle Booking

How it works: Browse, compare, and see who is closest on the live map. Instantly book lifestyle services — wellness, pet care, barbers.
The payoff: Choose the perfect provider by ratings, price, and schedule — on your own terms.

Business Model

Simple, transparent monetization across bidding, booking & advertising.

Take rate

Trade Commission

A percentage-based commission for India and fixed commission for the UK market on every completed bid-trade — charged to the pro on the winning quote.

Per booking

Service Fee

A flat service fee charged to the customer at checkout on both engines, on top of the winning bid or listed price.

Ad placement

Advertising Revenue

Home-screen & search placements sold to pros and category partners — a scalable, high-margin layer as usage grows.

Worked example below — exact commission and fee tiers to be finalized during pilot, priced to stay below traditional callout markups while sustaining unit economics.
